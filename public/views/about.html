About Randy Cooley

Randy has over 30 years of experience working on Ann Arbor area houses. He has extensive experience working on homes of all ages and styles, and can offer recommendations to modernize or keep the classic feel of your home. Randy is licensed and insured in the state of Michigan.

Randy offers many services in interior and exterior home repair and remodeling, specializing in bathroom and kitchen remodels with tile installation. Additionally, carpentry work, plaster and drywall repairs, floor plan alterations, and interior and exterior painting services are offered.
